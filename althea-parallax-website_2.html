The Name

Where healing
becomes a way of living.

Ἀλθαίνω — to heal, to restore,
to make whole again.

Althea takes its name from an ancient Greek verb — one that speaks of mending, renewal, of coming home to something more than walls and ceilings. In this spirit, every detail of the complex was composed to serve not just function, but the deeper quality of daily life.

Architecture, aesthetics, and lived experience coexist in precise harmony — a collection of residences designed to express exclusivity, refined aesthetic vision, and an authentic encounter with Mediterranean contemporary luxury.

The Architecture

A new order of
Mediterranean living

An entirely new residential complex with basement, pilotis, and two floors of curated typologies — designed with an uncompromising emphasis on natural light, spatial functionality, and the contemporary Mediterranean aesthetic.

A

Five refined single-level apartments ranging from intimate 35 m² studios to expansive 106 m² three-bedroom suites with Master Suite and walk-in wardrobe.

B

Six split-level residences with loft mezzanines and private rooftop pools — an elevated loft living experience unique in the Heraklion market.

Sun & Light

Light is the
first material

The orientation of every residence was calibrated to maximise natural light throughout the day — not as afterthought, but as primary architectural intention.

07:50 Morning light enters the east-facing living volumes — cool, directional, clarifying.
12:50 Midday light floods south-facing terraces; deep overhangs provide natural solar control.
17:50 Late afternoon: warm amber diffusion through west openings, long interior shadows.
19:50 Golden hour — the building holds its warmth longest in the upper loft terraces.
